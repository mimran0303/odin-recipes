Brownie Recipe

Description

Classic chocolate dessert that is a guranteed crowd pleaser!

Ingredients

  • flour
  • cocoa powder
  • sugar
  • eggs
  • butter

Directions:

  1. First sift dry ingredients
  2. In seperate bowl beat butter, sugar and eggs
  3. Mix wet and dry mixture.
  4. Bake at 350 degree farenheit for 30 minutes.
  5. Slice and enjoy!

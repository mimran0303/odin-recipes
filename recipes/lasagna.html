Lasagna Recipe

Description

This classic lasagna layers tender noodles with a rich, savory meat sauce made from ground beef and tomatoes. A creamy blend of ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan adds a cheesy, luxurious texture. Baked until golden and bubbly, this dish is the perfect comfort food for any occasion.

This lasagna is a delicious mix of robust meat sauce, tender noodles, and creamy cheeses. The sauce, simmered with Italian herbs, pairs perfectly with a ricotta and mozzarella filling. Topped with Parmesan and baked to perfection, it’s a comforting meal that’s sure to please.

Ingredients

  • lasagna sheets
  • meat
  • cheese
  • sauce
  • seasonings and herbs

Ingredients

  1. First boil the lasagna sheets
  2. Next add seasoning to the sauce
  3. Now layer sheets, sauce, meat, and cheese.
  4. Bake at 400 degree farenheit for 50 minutes.
  5. Slice and enjoy!
